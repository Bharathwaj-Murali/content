
#### Playbooks
##### New: User Investigation - Generic
This playbook performs an investigation on a specific user, using queries and logs from SIEM, Identity management systems, XDR, and firewalls.

Supported Integrations:
-Okta
-Splunk
-QRadar
-Azure Log Analytics
-PAN-OS
-XDR By Palo Alto Networks (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.5.0).
